LABUAN RUNNER SYSTEM

Website

Labuan Runner System (LRS) is a web-based system of booking dispatch parcel service. My focus to build this system for startup company of dispatch parcel service in Labuan.

The main objectives were to display quotation, send an email notification and improve application form. Basically, this website has 3 users which were for customer, dispatcher and admin.

Visit Website

HOW IT WORKS?

Customer

Customer need to fill the instant quote form and choose which service package that they want.

After that they will fill the booking form and pay the service by using paypal.

After the payment done, receipt will be sent to their email.

Admin

Admin able to manage the bookings that made by customer. Manage the task that accepted by the dispatcher (which dispatcher that accept bookings request).

Manage users (customer, admin & dispatcher), where they can add, preview and delete the user. Admin also can approve or disapprove application of dispatcher. Approvement notification will automatically sent to the dispatcher's email

Manage the service packages by add, edit or delete the packages. Lastly, admin also can send email directly through this website.

Dispatcher

Dispatcher can see bookings request through their wall.

They also can manage the process of dispatch parcel.

Plus, they also can view their history task.

PROBLEMS

I pick one dispatch parcel that work as a group in Labuan as my case study. Their management system basically using a whatsapp group as their main platform to communicate and distribute task/job to other members.

By using this method, there were always a human error happened during running this business. For example, fraud or scam in payment, inconsistency of price and notification during delivery, documents missing and limited number of member in whatsapp group.

Referred to these problems that facing by the company, I tried to build a web-based that could solved their problems

CHALLENGES

The challenges when to build this system was the company that I choose as my case study no longer operating. Since I could not find any dispatcher company in Labuan, I choose to referred a dispatcher that work in pair, group or solo in Labuan and Sabah to gather some information.

I also referred few existing websites dispatch parcel at peninsular Malaysia as reference. However, I was unable to make a bookings since the service only available at peninsular Malaysia, I also unable to sign-in into dispatcher's and admin's account.

Therefore, with information that I got from dispatchers and existing websites, I compare, combine and convert it into a website that could ease the customer as much as possible.

Research

To figure out the specifics users needs was a tricky part, especially for customer. To get a broad information about customer needs, I distribute questionnaire to potential customer by using a Google Form because it easy to use.

In order to get accurate answers and better ideas, I also do the interview session with a few potential customer and dispatcher. I more focus on asked about problem that have been faced and procedure they currently used.

I also do some application review to study about competitor's website design and features that they have in their website. After that, I compare and combine and apply it into this system so it can ease the users.
